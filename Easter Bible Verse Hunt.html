INSPIRED TO SPEAK: EASTER BIBLE VERSE HUNT

Team Challenge Instructions:

1. Work together to solve each clue

2. Find the Old Testament verse in your Bible app

3. Text the verse reference and how it connects to Easter to your Youth Leader

4. First team to text the correct answer gets 5 points!

1
Easter Clue: Find the Old Testament passage that prophesied Jesus would enter Jerusalem on a donkey, leading to shouts of joy.

This prophecy is found in one of the Minor Prophets whose name starts with "Z".

2
Easter Clue: Find the Old Testament passage that describes someone's hands and feet being pierced, and people casting lots for his clothing, 1000 years before crucifixion was invented.

This prophecy is found in a Psalm written by David.

3
Easter Clue: Find the Old Testament passage that describes a Suffering Servant who was "pierced for our transgressions" and by whose "wounds we are healed."

This prophecy is found in a book named after a major prophet who wrote around 700 BC.

4
Easter Clue: Find the Old Testament passage that gives a prophecy about the exact amount of money for which Jesus would be betrayed.

This prophecy mentions "thirty pieces of silver" and is found in a book named after a prophet whose name starts with "Z".

5
Easter Clue: Find the Old Testament passage that predicts the Messiah would be born in Bethlehem.

This prophecy is found in one of the Minor Prophets whose name starts with "M".

6
Easter Clue: Find the Old Testament passage that tells of a man who spent three days and nights in the belly of a great fish, foreshadowing Jesus' time in the tomb.

Jesus Himself referred to this story as a sign of His resurrection in Matthew 12:40.

7
Easter Clue: Find the Old Testament passage that describes a stone being rejected by builders but becoming the cornerstone.

This prophecy is found in a Psalm that is often quoted in the New Testament.

8
Easter Clue: Find the Old Testament passage that speaks of "one they have pierced" whom people will mourn for "as one mourns for an only child."

This prophecy is found in the last chapter of a book named after a prophet whose name starts with "Z".
